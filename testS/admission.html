Extract from the Order to issue invitations for study (internship) in Ukraine to the foreigners and persons without citizenship and their registration, approved with the order of the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine nr. 1541 of 01.11.2013

Invitations are issued to foreigners for whom there is given consent from an educational institution for admission to obtain the appropriate degree of higher, postgraduate education (advanced training, internship), postgraduate study, doctoral studies, clinical residency, medical residency, preparatory faculty, department (units), training on academic mobility programs or study the state language and / or language of teaching process.

Selection Committee provides recommendations on invitation of a foreigner to study on the basis of submitted copies of the following documents to an educational institution in paper or electronically:

  • Passport document of a foreigner;
  • Document on the obtained educational level with the received grades (points) on the educational discipline or academic aid;
  • Written agreement on the processing of personal data.

The educational institution determines the necessity of translation of the documents into Ukrainian, as well as conducting an interview, online if possible.

Extract from the Procedure to organize enrolment and training (internship) of foreigners and persons without citizenship, approved with the order of the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine nr. 1541 of 01.11.2013

Foreigners are enrolled into the educational institutions under the accredited educational programs:

  • Twice a year, before and at the beginning of the academic semesters (before November 1 and until March 1, respectively): to obtain degree of Junior Bachelor, Bachelor, Master (an educational degree of Junior Specialist) – based on the assessment / consideration of submited documents under sub-items 1, 2, clause 9, of this Procedure and enrolment tests on certain subjects and teaching language learning;
  • During the year for postgraduate, doctoral studies – based on the assessment / consideration of submited documents under sub-items 1, 2, 10, clause 9, of this Procedure and enrolment tests on certain subjects and teaching language learning.
    The educational institution carries out the calculation of grades / marks of the enrolment student on the basis of the document on previous education level and establishes the minimum necessary number of grades / marks of general educational disciplines, on which enrolment tests are carried out, for enrolment.
    Enrollment for studying to obtain higher education at the corresponding educational level is carried out on the results of the enrolment tests and on the basis of academic rights to continue education, which are given with a document on the educational level in the country of origin, and consideration of grades, which give the right to continue education at the next higher educational level in accordance with the legislation of the country, which has issued a document on the obtained educational level.
    Requirements of an educational institution on correspondence of the enrolled students from a number of foreigners to the enrolment requirements at the corresponding level of higher education and made public on the official web-site of the educational institution;
  • During the year to study on the programs of the preparatory faculty, department (unit), on study the state language and / or the language of the course of studies, as well as for obtaining postgraduate education, improving qualification, internship, in the order of transfer and renewal – on the basis of the decision of the enrolment committee of the educational institution on the results of studying the documents submitted in accordance with paragraph 9 of this the Order;

    • {Subparagraph 3, clause 6, as amended in accordance with the Order of the Ministry of Education and Science nr. 1167 of 11.08.2017}

  • During the year to study on the programs of academic mobility – in accordance with the Regulations on the procedure for the exercise of the right to academic mobility, approved by the Resolution of the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine nr. 579 of August 12, 2015;
  • During the year for training in clinical residency, medical residency – in accordance with the rules of admission to a higher educational institution.

    • Terms of submission, consideration / evaluation of documents, conducting enrolment tests and enrollment of foreigners are set by the rules of admission to a higher educational institution.

    Orders for enrollment of foreigners to study are issued by the head of the educational institution on the basis of the decision of the enrolment committee. Orders for admission to study with the appendices to them are formed in the Uniform state electronics database on educational matters.

    Admission of foreigners on training programs at preparatory faculties, departments (divisions), study of the state language and / or the language of instruction is carried out if they have a complete general secondary education, received outside Ukraine.

To be enrolled to the educational institution, the foreigner personally submits application in paper to the appropriate structural unit / admissions commission.

    With the application, a foreigner adds:

  • A document (an original and its copy) about the previous educational (educational-qualification) level, on the basis of which the enrolment is performed;
  • An application (an original and its copy) about the previous educational (educational-qualification) level, on the basis of which the enrolment is performed (if any);
  • An application (an original and its copy) about the previous educational (educational-qualification) level, on the basis of which the enrolment is performed (if any);
  • An original and a copy of the document, which contains the document on the curriculum on previous level of higher education, received credits, length of service and grades received on the educational disciplines (in case of absence of information annexed to the certificate of education) for enrollment to receive Master degree or postgraduate studies, if absence of this information makes qualification recognition on the document impossible;
  • Copy of the passport document of a foreigner or a document certifying a stateless person;
  • Medical insurance, unless otherwise provided by international treaties of Ukraine;
  • 4 photographs with a size of 30 x 40 mm;
  • Copy of the certificate of the foreign Ukrainian (if any);
  • Research proposal of the chosen scientific specialty or certified list of published scientific works and inventions in Ukrainian or in English in the prescribed manner by place of work / training of a candidate are added additionally at enrollment to the postgraduate study.

    • For enrollment to doctorate studies the following documents are required additionally in Ukrainian or English:

    Thematic plan for the thesis to obtain a doctoral degree;

    Copy of diploma on the award of the corresponding academic degree;

  • Foreigners, who are enrolled on the studying programs on academic mobility, submit documents approved by the rules of admission to a higher educational institution and / or foreseeing with the requirements of international programs and / or contracts on the implemented academic mobility.

Documents, specified in subparagraphs 1 to 5 of this paragraph must be translated into Ukrainian language with a notarized translation.

Documents referred to in subparagraphs 1 to 4 of this paragraph must be certified in the countries of their issue in the manner that is officially applied in this country for such certification, and legalized by the relevant foreign institution of Ukraine, unless otherwise provided by international agreements of Ukraine.
